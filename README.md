*Chapter 2*

## The Celery Organization

</div>

<br/>

[Celery](https://github.com/celery/celery) is one of the most widely-used distributed task queues in the world. Thousands of companies rely on it every day to process millions of tasks.

I am the **Owner** of the [Celery Organization](https://github.com/celery) on GitHub.

That means I manage the everyday activities of the ecosystem — reviewing code, guiding architectural decisions, mentoring contributors, and ensuring the project continues to serve the community well.

But owning an organization is just part of the story. The other part is building something new.

*Chapter 3*

## Building pytest-celery

</div>

<br/>

As **Tech-Lead** and **Creator** of [pytest-celery](https://github.com/celery/pytest-celery), I built the official pytest plugin for testing Celery applications from the ground up.

Before pytest-celery, integration testing for distributed task queues was painful. Now, developers can write clean, reliable tests with a familiar pytest interface.

This is the project closest to my heart.
