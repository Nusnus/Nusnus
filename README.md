## About Me

Hey there! I'm **Tomer Nosrati**, a.k.a **Nusnus** (pronounced "Noosnoos").

I'm a software engineer and the **tech-lead** of [pytest-celery](https://pytest-celery.readthedocs.io/). As an **owner** in the [Celery organization](https://github.com/celery), I manage the everyday activities of the organization in collaboration with the rest of the team to maintain the organization's projects and community.

> For consulting services, please [get in touch](mailto:tomer.nosrati@gmail.com). For more about me, visit my [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tomernosrati/).
